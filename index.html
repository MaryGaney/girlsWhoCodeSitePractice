Girls Who Code at HSE

Girls Who Code is a national organization that encourages young girls to pursue learning computer science and the world of coding. We have our own club here at HSE. If you would like to code with a group of awesome and supportive girls, please join our club!

Projects

Girls Go Cyberstart (CyberStart America)

We participated in this cybersecurity competition for girls and solved enough challenges to qualify for the national championships! We then participated in the national championships in May 2020.

Launch(ed) Women in Technology

We went to an event to present a guitar that we made with microbits.
